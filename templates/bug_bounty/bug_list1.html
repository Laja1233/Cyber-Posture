Program Guidelines & Rules

Please read and follow these guidelines carefully. Failure to comply may result in disqualification from the program.

  • Only test against systems explicitly listed in the program scope
  • Do not access, modify, or delete customer data or personal information
  • Do not perform any actions that could disrupt services or operations
  • Submit only original research that hasn't been publicly disclosed
  • Provide clear proof of concept without causing actual harm
  • Allow reasonable time for the organization to address the issue before public disclosure
  • Social engineering attacks against employees are strictly prohibited
  • Physical testing of facilities is not permitted without prior approval
  • Automated scanners must be rate-limited and non-intrusive
  • Multiple reports of the same vulnerability will be rewarded on a first-come basis
  • All testing must comply with applicable laws and regulations
  • Researchers must not attempt to access accounts that do not belong to them
  • Report vulnerabilities through official channels only
  • Do not publicly disclose vulnerabilities before they are resolved
  • Respect the privacy and security of other users and systems
